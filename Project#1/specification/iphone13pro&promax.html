Generation 17.2: iPhone 13 Pro and iPhone 13 Pro Max

Release Date: September 14, 2021

Apple released the iPhone 13 in 2021, following the same pattern as the iPhone 12 release in 2020. Both releases featured two different versions: the iPhone 13 and iPhone 13 mini, and the iPhone 13 Pro and iPhone 13 Pro Max. The iPhone 13 and iPhone 13 mini are the more affordable models in the lineup, while the iPhone 13 Pro and iPhone 13 Pro Max are the more high-end models. Both lineups feature a number of new features, such as a Cinematic mode for video recording and a longer battery life.
