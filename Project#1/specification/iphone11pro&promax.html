Generation 15.2: iPhone 11 Pro and iPhone 11 Pro Max

Release Date: September 20, 2019

Apple also announced two more iPhones in 2019: the iPhone 11 Pro and the iPhone 11 Pro Max. These phones are more expensive than the iPhone 11, but they have some additional features. The iPhone 11 Pro and Pro Max have the same A13 Bionic processor as the iPhone 11, but they have a longer battery life. The iPhone 11 Pro is designed to last an additional 4 hours longer than the iPhone XS, and the iPhone 11 Pro Max is designed to last an additional 5 hours longer than the iPhone XS Max. The iPhone 11 Pro and Pro Max also have OLED Super Retina displays, while the iPhone 11 has an LCD Liquid Crystal display. OLED displays have better color reproduction and contrast than LCD displays. The iPhone 11 Pro starts at $999, and the iPhone 11 Pro Max starts at $1,099. They are a good option for people who want the latest iPhone with the best features.
