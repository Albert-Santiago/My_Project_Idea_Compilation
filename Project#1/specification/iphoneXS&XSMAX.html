Generation 14.1: iPhone XS and iPhone XS Max

Release Date: September 21, 2018:

The iPhone XS and XS Plus are the latest iPhones from Apple. They are essentially improved versions of the iPhone X, with a faster processor, a better camera, and a more water-resistant design. The iPhone XS has a 5.8-inch screen, while the iPhone XS Plus has a 6.5-inch screen. Both phones have a bezel-less design, and they use Face ID for facial recognition. The iPhone XS and XS Plus are powered by the A12 Bionic chip, which is the fastest chip ever used in a smartphone. They also have a new dual-camera system on the back, which takes better photos in low-light conditions. The iPhone XS and XS Plus are both water-resistant up to 2 meters for up to 30 minutes. This means that you can use them in the rain or even take them swimming. The iPhone XS and XS Plus are available in three colors: silver, gold, and space gray. They start at $999 for the iPhone XS and $1,099 for the iPhone XS Plus.
