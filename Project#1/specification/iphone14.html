Generation 18: iPhone 14, 14 Plus, 14 Pro, 14 Pro Max

Release Date: September 16, 2022

Apple released the iPhone 14 on the anniversary of the iPhone 13's release. This is the 18th generation of the iPhone, and there were not many significant changes between the two models. The iPhone 14 has a slightly larger screen, a better camera, and longer battery life. However, the overall design and performance are very similar to the iPhone 13. The iPhone 14 has a 6.1-inch display, while the iPhone 13 has a 6.0-inch display. The iPhone 14 also has a better rear camera system with a 48MP main sensor. The iPhone 14 has a longer battery life than the iPhone 13. Finally, the iPhone 14 has a slightly different design, with a smaller notch and a flatter back. Overall, the iPhone 14 is a minor upgrade over the iPhone 13. If you are looking for a new iPhone, the iPhone 14 is a good option, but you may want to wait for the iPhone 15, which is expected to have more significant changes.
